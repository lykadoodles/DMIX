Your Architectural Roadmap

The "Sovereign System Architect" Persona

Hello [Integrator Name],

Thank you for completing our questionnaire. Your focus on architectural control, platform independence, and minimizing ongoing fees identifies you as a **Sovereign System Architect**. This path provides the ultimate control over your integration.

Critical Considerations First

The **Direct Integration (Raw TCP to 3rd Party Server)** path is our most advanced. By bypassing Device Manager for data, you assume responsibility for services it typically provides. Please carefully consider:

  • GNSS Aiding Data: Bypassing DM can slow down GPS fix times and increase battery usage.
  • FOTA Updates: Devices **must** be instructed to reconnect to Device Manager to receive critical firmware updates.
  • Support: Our ability to diagnose device communication issues is limited without Device Manager's logs.

Your 3-Step Implementation Plan

  1. Initial Provisioning: In Device Manager, configure your device's Admin Parameters to point to your server's IP and port. This is a one-time setup step per device.
  2. Server Implementation: Build your TCP server to handle the full, stateful device communication protocol (Hello, Data, Commit, Async, etc.). This is more complex than a simple data listener.
  3. FOTA & Management Strategy: We recommend using the "Periodic DM Connection" setting or implementing the async message (`0x0007`) to instruct devices to check in for firmware updates.

Your Essential Documentation

These documents are non-negotiable for the direct integration path.

  • The Core Protocol: [Attachment] Direct Third Party Server Communication 1.8.pdf **(YOUR MOST IMPORTANT DOCUMENT)**
  • Payload Parsing: [Attachment] DMT Data Fields v8.21.pdf
  • Event Decoding: [Attachment] DMT Log Reasons.pdf

What You Can Safely Ignore

  • Device Manager Connectors (JSON & TCP) as you are bypassing them.
  • Most of the OEM WebAPI, unless you use it to manage the periodic check-in parameters.

This path offers maximum control. We recommend starting with a TCP Connector in Device Manager to validate your parser before switching the device to direct mode.
